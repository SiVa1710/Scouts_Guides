Welcome to the **Scouts & Guides** app—your all-in-one digital companion designed specifically for dedicated Scouts and Guides! Whether you are preparing for your Rajyapuraskar testing, practicing your daily routines, or logging your good deeds, this app provides everything you need in a modern, easy-to-use interface.

## ✨ Core Features

* **🧘‍♂️ Meditation & Prayer Room:** Practice the official Scouts & Guides prayer with our interactive audio player. Includes synchronized background audio, a real-time visual waveform spectrum analyzer, and an integrated devotional lyrics sheet.
* **⏱️ Rajyapuraskar Exam Tools:** Features a built-in 90-second exam timer and floating rule tooltips to help you perfect your testing requirements.
* **🇮🇳 National Anthem & Flag Song:** High-quality audio tracks accompanied by complete lyrics, ensuring you are always ready for official ceremonies and campfires.
* **📖 Good Turn Diary:** A digital responsive log to track your daily good deeds. Keep your promises organized and reflect on your journey.
* **🎨 Immersive UI:** A hardware-accelerated user interface featuring a stunning dark theme, soothing gradient backgrounds, and fast performance.
* **📶 Offline Accessibility:** Practice your songs and read your rules even when you are out camping in the wilderness.

## 🎯 Who is this for?
The perfect pocket resource for **Cubs, Bulbuls, Scouts, Guides, Rovers, Rangers, and Scoutmasters** who want quick access to essential audio tracks, lyrics, and daily logging tools.
